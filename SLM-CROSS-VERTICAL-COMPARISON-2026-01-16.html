1. SLM Landscape Overview (2025-2026)

Market Dynamics

The SLM market represents a fundamental shift in enterprise AI adoption. While large language models (LLMs) dominated 2023-2024, 2025 marks the "Year of Small Models" as organizations prioritize cost, privacy, and deployment flexibility.

Market Drivers

  • Cost Efficiency: SLMs are 1,000-10,000x cheaper per token than reasoning LLMs
  • Privacy/Compliance: On-device processing eliminates third-party data exposure (critical for GDPR, HIPAA)
  • Latency: Edge deployment delivers <50ms response times vs. 200-500ms cloud latency
  • Hardware Acceleration: NPUs, Apple M-series chips, and RTX GPUs enable local SLM inference
  • Fine-Tuning Economics: Open weights + parameter-efficient methods democratize vertical AI
