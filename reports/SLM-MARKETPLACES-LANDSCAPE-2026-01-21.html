7 Critical Marketplace Gaps

1. Multi-LoRA Adapter Marketplace

Gap: No dedicated marketplace for discovering, sharing, and monetizing LoRA adapters across base models. Predibase/Together AI have multi-LoRA serving tech but not a public marketplace.

Opportunity: Create adapter registry with versioning, compatibility tags, and crypto micropayments. Revenue: 5-10% transaction fees.

2. Vertical-Specific SLM Discovery

Gap: Discovering domain-specific SLMs (medical, legal, DeFi) requires manual search across HuggingFace, GitHub, papers. No unified vertical registry.

Opportunity: Curated vertical SLM marketplaces with quality scores, benchmark comparisons, compliance certifications.

3. DeFi/Crypto SLM Registry

Gap: While DeFAI agents exist, no marketplace specifically for crypto-specialized SLMs (token analysis, yield optimization, sentiment analysis).

Opportunity: Crypto-native SLM marketplace with tokenized ownership, on-chain provenance, automated royalty distribution.

4. Edge SLM Distribution

Gap: Despite $5.45B market projection, no streamlined marketplace for edge-optimized SLMs with device compatibility metadata.

Opportunity: Edge SLM store with device profiles (iOS, Android, IoT), model size filters, inference benchmarks.

5. Transparent Fine-Tuning Pricing

Gap: Monster API, Gradient AI, OctoAI post-acquisition lack transparent pricing. Requires sales calls or opaque credit systems.

Opportunity: Calculator tools and transparent pricing comparison sites for fine-tuning services.

6. Model Performance Leaderboards by Vertical

Gap: General leaderboards exist (HuggingFace Open LLM), but vertical-specific benchmarks (legal accuracy, DeFi prediction) are scattered.

Opportunity: Vertical-specific leaderboards with standardized benchmarks for domain tasks.

7. Governance and Discovery at Scale

Gap: 60-70% of AI pilots fail to reach production due to governance gaps. Enterprises lack AI-ready data and contextual model discovery.

Opportunity: Enterprise model catalog with governance metadata (data lineage, bias reports, compliance tags).
