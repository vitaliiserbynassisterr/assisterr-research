Top 5 Complaints About Paid Offerings

  1. Hidden infrastructure costs exceed training costs - Serving is 5-10x more expensive than training
  2. Model degradation when base models update - Forces complete retraining
  3. Fine-tuning costs don't guarantee quality - RAG might have been cheaper
  4. Proprietary lock-in creates forced upgrades - OpenAI 6x more expensive for fine-tuned models
  5. Complex deployment & serving challenges - Setup harder than fine-tuning itself

Key Insight

Developers don't pay for fine-tuning tools.

They pay for making fine-tuning EASY and CHEAP at scale. The winning play: infrastructure abstraction, deployment simplification, cost predictability.
